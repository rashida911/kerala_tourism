Idukki

Idukki is a district located in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It is a popular destination for tourists who are looking for a peaceful and refreshing experience in the lap of nature. Here are some of the top tourist attractions in Idukki:

Idukki Arch Dam:

This is one of the largest arch dams in Asia and a must-visit for anyone coming to Idukki. The dam is located between two hills and provides a breathtaking view of the surrounding landscape.

Munnar:

This hill station is located in Idukki and is known for its scenic beauty and tea plantations. It is a popular destination for honeymooners and nature lovers.

Anamudi Peak:

This is the highest peak in South India and is located in the Eravikulam National Park. Visitors can take a trek to the top of the peak to enjoy a panoramic view of the surrounding area.

Mattupetty Dam:

This dam is located in Munnar and is a popular picnic spot. Visitors can also take a boat ride on the lake and enjoy the scenic beauty of the surrounding hills.

Vagamon:

This hill station is known for its rolling hills, green meadows, and pine forests. It is a popular destination for trekking and camping.

These are just a few of the many tourist attractions in Idukki. Whether you are looking for a relaxing vacation or an adventure-packed holiday, Idukki has something to offer for everyone.
