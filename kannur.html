Kasargod

Kannur is a beautiful city located in the northern part of the Indian state of Kerala. It is known for its pristine beaches, historical monuments, cultural heritage, and delicious cuisine. Here are some of the top tourist attractions in Kannur:

Payyambalam Beach:

This is one of the most popular beaches in Kannur, known for its golden sand and clear blue water. Visitors can enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and water sports here.

St. Angelo Fort:

This historical fort was built by the Portuguese in the 16th century and is a major tourist attraction in Kannur. It offers stunning views of the Arabian Sea and the surrounding landscape.

Muzhappilangad Beach:

This is the only drive-in beach in Kerala and is a must-visit for all adventure enthusiasts. Visitors can enjoy driving along the 4 km long stretch of sand, which is surrounded by coconut groves and the Arabian Sea.

Parassinikadavu Temple:

This is a famous Hindu temple located on the banks of the Valapattanam River. The temple is dedicated to the goddess Muthappan and is known for its traditional rituals and cultural performances.

Theyyam:

This is a popular folk art form that originated in North Kerala and is performed during the festival season. Visitors can witness the vibrant and colorful performances that showcase the rich cultural heritage of the region.

Overall, Kannur is a wonderful destination for anyone who is looking for a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and delicious cuisine.
